Chicken Adobo

Description

Adobo is a traditional Filipino comfort food that is consisted of vinegar and soysauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Dried Bay Leaves
  • Soy Sauce
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Water
  • Cooking Oil
  • Teaspoon of sugar
  • Teaspoon of salt
  • Teaspoon of whole peppercorn

Steps to follow

  1. Combine the chicken, soy sauce and garlic in a bowl. Marinate at least 1 hour.

  2. Heat a cooking pot and pour cooking oil.

  3. When the oil is hot enough, pan fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes

  4. Pour in the remaining marinade and add water. Bring in to a boil.

  5. Add in bay leaves and whole peppercorn. Simmer for at least 30 minutes.

  6. Add sugar or salt depending on your preferred taste.

  7. Serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
