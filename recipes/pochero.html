Chicken Pochero

Description

Chicken Pochero is a chicken stew recipe that consists of tomato sauce and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Potato
  • Bok Choy
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Banana (Saba)
  • Cooking Oil

Steps to follow

  1. Heat oil in cooking pot and pan fry the chicken 2 minutes per side.

  2. Using the remaining oil saute onion, garlic and tomatoes.

  3. Pour tomato sauce and water and let it boil for 20 - 30 minutes. or until the chicken softens.

  4. Pour in potatoes, banana(saba) and let it boil for 12 minutes.

  5. Add long green beans, bok choy and cabbange. Let it cook for 3 minutes.

  6. Season with salt and peppercorn depending on your preferred taste.

  7. Serve with hot rice and Enjoy!
