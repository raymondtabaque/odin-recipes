Chicken Kare Kare

Description

Chicken Kare Kare is another variation of the Kare Kare Filipino recipe. Instead of using pork or beef we will be using chicken in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Peanut Butter
  • Bok Choy
  • Egg plant
  • Snake beans
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chicken Broth
  • Cooking Oil

Steps to follow

  1. Heat oil in cooking pot.

  2. Saute onion and garlic until onion softens.

  3. Add chicken slices. Cook each side for 1 to 1/2 minutes. Continue to cook and saute the chicken for 2 more minutes.

  4. Pour chicken broth and let boil. Let it boil for 30 - 35 minutes.

  5. Add peanut butter. Stir until well blended. Note: add more peanut butter if the sauce needs to be thickened further.

  6. Put the eggplant and snake beans into the pot. Cover and cook for 5 minutes. Add bok choy and cook for 3 minutes.

  7. Serve with your favorite bagoong and Enjoy!
